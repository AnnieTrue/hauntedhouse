All-Time Favorite
I've been going here with my family for years and i was always too scared to actually go in until a few of years ago. for the past 4 or 5 years, I've been making time to visit this haunted house every year and every time I'm more and more terrified and impressed. i was most impressed this year. the actors are great, the makeup is awesome, the effects they use are perfect! you experience moments where you don't know where your going or whats lurking around you or in front of you, for instance just pushing through layers of black sheets that feel almost endless (the suspense killed me). theres so many different types of scenes you go through and each one was perfectly decorated. it seemed more natural than other haunts such as bayville scream park, that just slather a bunch of props into a room and stick a random actor in there. dark side has a more professional and effective way of making you feel like you're really trapped and almost as if the scenes you are walking through are coming to life. its so scary. it was the only haunted house that got my boyfriend to say he was truly scared, even after we had gone to Terror Behind the Walls at Eastern State Pennitentiary, Gateway's Haunted Playhouse and Shmitts farm's haunts. This was my favorite and I'm so glad i went again this year. GREAT JOB and thank you to everybody who works on this haunt, the actors, makeup, props and everything are absolutely perfect!!
